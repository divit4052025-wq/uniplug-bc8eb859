# UniPlug — Privacy Policy

**Data Fiduciary:** UniPlug Technologies Private Limited ("**UniPlug**", "**we**", "**us**")
**Last updated:** 30 May 2026 · **Version 1.0**

> This Privacy Policy explains how UniPlug collects, uses, shares and protects personal data, and your rights under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act")** and other applicable Indian law. Because many of our users are children, protecting their data is central to how we operate.

---

## 2. The personal data we collect

**You give us:**
- **Account & identity:** name, email address, **date of birth**, role (Student / Mentor), password (stored only in hashed form by our auth provider).
- **For students under 18:** your **Parent's name and email address**, collected so we can obtain verifiable parental consent.
- **Profile & guidance data:** grade/year, target colleges, interests, and material you choose to submit such as **essays and documents** for review.
- **Mentor data:** college/university, verification documents and details, profile and availability.
- **Communications:** messages you send through the Platform and support requests.

**Created when you use the Platform:**
- **Booking & session data:** sessions booked, attended, cancelled, and a record that a video room was joined and by whom (a safeguarding audit log). **We do not record or transcribe the content of video calls.**
- **Payment data:** transaction records (processed by Razorpay; we do not store full card or bank details).
- **Technical data:** limited log and device information needed to keep you logged in, operate the service and keep it secure.

We collect only what we need to run the service.

## 3. Why we use your data and on what basis

| Purpose | Examples | Legal basis (DPDP Act) |
|---|---|---|
| Provide the service | create your account, match and book sessions, run video calls | Consent / performance of the service requested |
| Obtain and record parental consent for minors | contact the Parent, record the consent decision | Consent (parental, for children) / legal obligation |
| Process payments | take payment, pay Mentors, handle refunds | Consent / performance; legal obligation for records |
| AI features you choose to use | essay review, FAQ, shortlisting | Consent |
| Safety & safeguarding | verify Mentors, keep a join-audit, investigate concerns | Legitimate uses / legal obligation |
| Communications | booking confirmations, reminders, service notices | Consent / performance |
| Security, fraud prevention, legal compliance | protect accounts, comply with law | Legal obligation / legitimate uses |

We will ask for fresh consent before using your data for a materially new purpose.

## 4. Children's data (users under 18)

4.1 UniPlug knowingly serves children, so we apply additional protections required by the DPDP Act:

- We **obtain verifiable consent from a Parent** before a child's personal data is processed for booking or attending Sessions.
- We **do not undertake tracking, behavioural monitoring, or targeted advertising directed at children.**
- We **do not process a child's data in a way likely to cause harm** to the child, as far as we can reasonably ensure.

4.2 A Parent can review, or ask us to correct or delete, their child's data, and can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 8).

4.3 More detail on the consent process is set out in the **Parental / Guardian Consent Terms**.

## 5. Who we share data with (processors / Data Processors)

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers who process it on our behalf, under contract, to run the Platform:

| Provider | Purpose | Where it processes/stores data |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Database, accounts and authentication (this holds most personal data, including essays and Parent contact details) | **Tokyo, Japan** |
| **Razorpay** | Payments | **India** (payment data localised in India) |
| **Cloudflare** | Website hosting, content delivery and edge processing | **Global edge network** (operated from outside India in part) |
| **Daily.co** | One-to-one video calls | **Global media servers** (largely outside India) |
| **Resend** | Transactional emails (confirmations, reminders, consent links) | **United States** |
| **Anthropic** | AI features you choose to use (e.g. essay review) | **United States** (processing may also occur in other regions) |

We may also disclose data where required by law, to protect safety, or in connection with a corporate transaction (with appropriate safeguards).

## 6. Transfer of data outside India

Because of the providers above, **some of your personal data is processed or stored outside India** (for example, our database is hosted in Japan, and email and AI services operate from the United States). We rely on your consent for these transfers and take reasonable steps to require our providers to protect your data. We do not transfer personal data to any country that the Government of India has restricted for such transfers. Payment data is kept in India in line with Reserve Bank of India requirements.

## 7. How long we keep data

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above. In general:
- **Active accounts:** while your account is open.
- **After account closure:** we delete or anonymise personal data within a reasonable period (target: 90 days), **except** data we must keep longer by law — for example, payment and tax records (retained for the period required under Indian financial and tax law) and safeguarding records where retention is necessary. *[Retention periods — confirm with counsel.]*

## 8. Your rights

Subject to the DPDP Act, you (and a Parent on behalf of a child) have the right to:
- **access** a summary of the personal data we process about you;
- **correct, complete or update** your data;
- **erase** your data where it is no longer needed;
- **withdraw consent** at any time (this does not affect processing already carried out, and may mean we can no longer provide some or all of the service);
- **grievance redressal** — raise a complaint with us; and
- **nominate** another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.

To exercise any right, email [privacy@uniplug.app](mailto:privacy@uniplug.app). We may need to verify your identity. We will respond within the timelines required by law.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may complain to the **Data Protection Board of India**.

## 9. How we protect your data

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit, access controls, row-level security on our database, and limiting staff access. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and will notify you and the Data Protection Board of a personal-data breach as required by law.

## 10. Cookies and similar technologies

We use only **essential storage** needed to keep you logged in and operate the service. We do **not** currently use third-party analytics or advertising trackers. See the **Cookie Policy** for details.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. If changes are material, we will notify you (for example, by email or in-app notice) before they take effect.
