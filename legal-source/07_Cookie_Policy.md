# UniPlug — Cookie & Local Storage Policy

**Operated by:** UniPlug Technologies Private Limited ("**UniPlug**")
**Last updated:** 30 May 2026 · **Version 1.0**

> This Policy explains the small amount of storage UniPlug uses in your browser. It forms part of our Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. What we use

UniPlug uses only **essential storage** — cookies and/or browser local storage that are strictly necessary to:
- keep you securely **logged in** and maintain your session;
- remember basic preferences needed for the Platform to work;
- keep the service secure.

Without this essential storage, core features such as logging in would not work.

## 2. What we do **not** use

We do **not** currently use:
- third-party **analytics** (such as Google Analytics);
- **advertising** or marketing trackers;
- social-media tracking pixels;
- cross-site behavioural tracking.

We do not track or profile users — and we do not behaviourally track or target children — for advertising.

## 3. Third-party providers

Some essential functionality is provided by our service providers (for example, our authentication and video providers). They may set storage strictly needed to deliver that feature. We do not use them to advertise to you. See our **Privacy Policy** for the full list of providers and where they process data.

## 4. Managing storage

Because we use only essential storage, there is no advertising or analytics to opt out of. You can clear or block cookies and site storage in your browser settings, but doing so may stop you from logging in or using parts of the Platform.

## 5. Changes

If we ever introduce analytics or other non-essential storage, we will update this Policy and, where the law requires, ask for your consent first.
