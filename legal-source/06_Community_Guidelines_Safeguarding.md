# UniPlug — Community Guidelines & Safeguarding Code

**Operated by:** UniPlug Technologies Private Limited ("**UniPlug**")
**Last updated:** 30 May 2026 · **Version 1.0**

> UniPlug connects school students — many of them minors — with college-student Mentors. Keeping students safe is our highest priority. These Guidelines apply to everyone on the Platform and form part of the Terms of Service. Breaking them can lead to removal from the Platform and, where appropriate, referral to the authorities.

---

## 1. Our safeguarding principles

- A student's safety and wellbeing come before everything else.
- Interaction happens **on the Platform**, where protections apply.
- Adults must never use the Platform to harm, exploit, groom or take advantage of a minor.

## 2. Rules for everyone

You must:
- treat others with respect — no harassment, bullying, threats, hate speech or discrimination;
- keep all communication and Sessions **on the Platform**;
- be honest about who you are, your age and your background.

You must **not**:
- share, request or collect another User's personal contact details (phone, social media, home address), or move the conversation off-Platform;
- **record, screen-capture or transcribe** any Session or message;
- share content that is sexual, violent, illegal, hateful, or otherwise harmful;
- ask anyone to send money, gifts or payment outside the Platform.

## 3. Additional rules for Mentors working with minors

If you are a Mentor, you must:
- keep every interaction strictly professional and focused on college guidance;
- **never** engage in romantic, sexual or otherwise inappropriate conduct or conversation with a Student — regardless of the Student's age or apparent wishes;
- **never** ask a Student to keep secrets from their parents or guardians, or try to isolate them from trusted adults;
- never request personal photos of a Student, or a Student's location or schedule beyond what a Session needs;
- never arrange to meet a Student in person;
- report any concern about a Student's safety immediately.

These rules exist to protect children and are treated with zero tolerance.

## 4. How we keep students safe

- **Mentor verification** before a Mentor can offer Sessions.
- **No call recording**, and a prohibition on Users recording.
- A **join-audit log** recording who joined each Session, kept for safeguarding.
- **Parental consent** required for every user under 18.
- The ability for Students and Parents to **report, block and end** a Session at any time.

## 5. Reporting a concern

If something feels wrong, or you are worried about a student's safety:
- use the in-app report option where available; or
- email **[safeguarding@uniplug.app](mailto:safeguarding@uniplug.app)** with details.

In an emergency, or if a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services and, in India, you can also reach the national child helpline by dialling **1098 (Childline)**.

We take every report seriously, act promptly, and may suspend an account while we investigate. We will not penalise anyone for reporting a genuine concern in good faith.

## 6. Consequences

Breaking these Guidelines can result in warnings, suspension, permanent removal, forfeiture of pending payouts (for Mentors), and referral to law-enforcement or child-protection authorities where required.
