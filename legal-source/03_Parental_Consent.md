# UniPlug — Parental / Guardian Consent

**Provided by:** UniPlug Technologies Private Limited ("**UniPlug**", "**we**", "**us**")
**Last updated:** 30 May 2026 · **Consent version:** 1.0

> Your child has registered, or wishes to register, on UniPlug. Because your child is under 18, the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** requires us to obtain your verifiable consent as their parent or lawful guardian before we process their personal data to provide our services. This page explains what you are being asked to agree to. Please read it before giving consent.

---

## 1. What UniPlug is

UniPlug connects school students in India with current college students ("**Mentors**") for one-to-one guidance on college applications. Guidance is peer experience, **not** professional counselling, and UniPlug does **not** guarantee any admission or other outcome.

## 2. What you are consenting to

By giving consent, you allow UniPlug to process your child's personal data so that your child can use the Platform, including to:

- create and operate your child's account;
- let your child browse Mentors, book and attend one-to-one Sessions;
- enable in-session video calls between your child and a Mentor;
- process essays or documents your child chooses to submit for review (including AI-assisted review your child requests);
- take payment for Sessions (through our payment partner, Razorpay); and
- send service messages such as booking confirmations and reminders.

## 3. The data we process about your child

- Name, email address and date of birth;
- Your name and email address (as the consenting Parent/Guardian);
- Profile and guidance information your child provides (e.g. grade, target colleges, essays);
- Booking and session records, and a safeguarding log recording that a video room was joined and by whom.

**We do not record or transcribe the video calls themselves.** Some of this data is processed by service providers outside India (see our **Privacy Policy**).

## 4. Safety measures for your child

- Mentors are subject to UniPlug's verification process before they can offer Sessions.
- Users are not permitted to record Sessions or to contact your child outside the Platform.
- We keep an audit record of who joined each session for safeguarding purposes.
- You can read how we keep children safe in the **Community Guidelines & Safeguarding Code**.

## 5. How consent is given and recorded

We obtain your consent through a secure link sent to your email address. When you confirm, we record your decision, the date and time, and the version of this consent text, as an immutable record. We may take reasonable steps to verify that you are an adult and the child's parent or guardian.

## 6. Your rights as a Parent / Guardian

At any time you may:
- **review** the personal data we hold about your child;
- ask us to **correct or delete** it;
- **withdraw your consent** — after which your child will no longer be able to book or attend Sessions, and we will stop processing their data except where we must keep records by law.

To exercise any of these, email [privacy@uniplug.app](mailto:privacy@uniplug.app) from the email address you used to give consent.

## 7. If you do not consent

If you do not give consent, your child's account will remain limited: they will not be able to book or attend Sessions, and we will not process their personal data for those purposes.
