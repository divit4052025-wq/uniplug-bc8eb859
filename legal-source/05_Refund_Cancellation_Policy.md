# UniPlug — Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Operated by:** UniPlug Technologies Private Limited ("**UniPlug**")
**Last updated:** 30 May 2026 · **Version 1.0**

> This Policy explains when you can cancel a Session or subscription and when you are entitled to a refund. It forms part of the Terms of Service. *[Note: the specific windows and amounts below are provisional and will be confirmed when pricing is finalised.]*

---

## 1. Payments

All payments are made through the Platform using our payment partner, Razorpay. Prices are shown before you pay and are in Indian Rupees (₹) unless stated otherwise.

## 2. Cancelling a Session — by the Student

2.1 **If you cancel 24 hours or more before** the Session's scheduled start time, you receive a **full refund or credit**.

2.2 **If you cancel less than 24 hours before** the Session, the Session fee is **non-refundable**, because the Mentor's time has been reserved. You may, where the Mentor agrees, reschedule instead.

2.3 Refunds are made to your original payment method, or as Platform credit if you prefer.

## 3. If the Mentor cancels or does not attend

If a Mentor cancels, or fails to attend within a reasonable time of the scheduled start, you are entitled to a **full refund** or a **free reschedule**, at your choice.

## 4. Technical failure

If a Session cannot take place or is materially disrupted because of a technical failure on UniPlug's side (not your own device or connection), you are entitled to a **full refund** or a **free reschedule**.

## 5. Subscriptions

5.1 Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled.

5.2 You may **cancel at any time**. Cancellation stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the current paid period.

5.3 Subscription fees already paid for the current period are **not refunded on a pro-rata basis**, except where required by law.

## 6. How to request a refund

Email [support@uniplug.app](mailto:support@uniplug.app) from your registered email, with your booking details. We aim to acknowledge within 24 hours and to process approved refunds within **5–7 business days** (timing may depend on Razorpay and your bank).

## 7. Platform fee

Where a refund is due, UniPlug processes the refund of the amount you paid. Mentor payouts are adjusted accordingly.

## 8. Your statutory rights

Nothing in this Policy limits any non-excludable rights you have as a consumer under Indian law, including the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
